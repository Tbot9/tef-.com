OUR MISSION

TEF is a non-profit organization with a mandate to provide quality education, especially to the marginalized communities, such as the rural poor and women.

OUR VISION

Our objective is to create centres of learning, which provide students an enabling
environment to develop their faculties of critical analysis and independent thinking,
to polish their communication and articulation skills, to increase their understanding
of social issues, and to make them active.
